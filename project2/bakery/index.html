We've been Making The Delicious Bread Since 2008

Founded in 2008 Baking Dreams is a fantastic purpose-built Cookery School and Foodie Event Space, based in Crawley, benefiting from easy transport links and ample parking. Alongside a specialism of innovative corporate and team building events, the team at Baking Dreams provide a wide variety of experiences including hands-on cookery classes, healthy eating, parents cooking with their children, barista training, informative nutrition courses and Baking Dreams own unique twist on going out for dinner where learning and eating out blend into a sociable evening.

To say we were thrilled is an understatement, we have won two awards as well as being awarded a certificate of excellence due to our 5 star reviews on Tripadvisor
