About me

A warm welcome from Photographica

My name is Ivan .I live in the lovely& friendly town Crawley ,West Sussex with my family and I breathe through photography.I use photography to communicate with the world and to create beauty,keep memories alive and tell stories as well as to inspire.My great ambition is to create beautiful images that commemorate, across generations, the people we love and the good times we have had . My style is relaxed and natural.I am using exclusively branded Nikon equipment, which delivers excellent results without a failure. I have a Diploma from ‘ Photography Course London’ based in the most artistic London’s neighbourhood Shoreditch, and lots of passion for photography. I love to travel and to explore new places.My father gave me my first camera-Taxona, when I was 7 years old and I quickly became the family historian.He taught me how to manually meter and set up the aperture. The photos I took through the years have become my precious treasure taking me back to my childhood, keeping memories of some family celebrations and special moments.
